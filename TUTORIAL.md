# How to Play

The goal of the game is to stop enemies from reaching the end of the path. Each enemy that reaches the end will cause you to lose one health point. If your health reaches zero, the game is over.

---

# Building Turrets

To build a turret, click one of the turret buttons at the bottom of the screen. There are three base turrets you can build:
- **Duo:** Shoots basic projectiles.
- **Lancer:** Shoots a fast laser bolt.
- **Wave:** Creates a field that slows down enemies.

After selecting a turret, you can place it on any available buildable tile on the map. Building turrets costs money, which you earn by defeating enemies.

---

# Upgrading and Selling Turrets

To upgrade or sell a turret, you must first exit build mode by pressing the `ESC` key.

Once you are out of build mode, click on a turret you have already placed on the map. A menu will appear on the right side of the screen with options to upgrade or sell the turret.

- **Upgrade:** Makes the turret stronger, but costs money.
- **Sell:** Removes the turret and gives you some of your money back.

---

# Enemy Waves

Enemies will attack in waves. To start the next wave, click the "Next Wave" button. You must defeat all enemies in the current wave to earn money and complete the wave.

# Enemy types

The main enemy types are are 6
- Flare : basic grunt enemy
- Mono : fast enemy
- Crawler : invisible enemy, reveal to turrets using slowing turrets, wave and salvo
- Locus : tank enemy, lots of health
- Poly : healer enemy, heals every enemy within certain range
